FreeBreath – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

FreeBreath ("the App") is a personal quit-smoking companion developed by Oscar Jorge Castillo. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects and how it is used.

Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. The App does not require an account, does not connect to the internet, and does not transmit any information to external servers.

All data you enter (number of cigarettes, pack price, quit date, personal goal) is stored exclusively on your device using iOS local storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device.

Data Stored on Your Device

  • Number of cigarettes smoked per day (entered by you)
  • Price per pack (entered by you)
  • Quit date and time (entered by you)
  • Personal savings goal (optional, entered by you)
  • Notification preferences
  • History of previous quit attempts (if you use the relapse feature)
  • Language preference

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Notifications

If you enable daily motivational notifications, these are scheduled locally on your device using iOS local notifications. No data is sent to any server for this purpose.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 years of age.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
